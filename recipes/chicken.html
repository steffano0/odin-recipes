Chicken Rice Bowl

Description:

A classic rice bowl of chicken and vegetables. A healthy option to eat on busy days.

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup of rice
  • cup of chicken broth
  • cup chopped frozen green pepper, thawed
  • cup chopped onion
  • 2 teaspoons olive oil
  • 1 package of ready to use grilled chicken breast strips or chicken breast
  • 1/2 cup frozen corn, thawed
  • 1/2 cup frozen peas, thawed
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • 1 teaspoon rubbed sage
  • 1/8 teaspoon salt
  • 1/8 teaspoon pepper

Steps:

  1. Cook the rice
  2. Saute the green pepper and onion in oli for 2-3 minutes
  3. Stir the chicken with oli or butter
  4. Stir in the chicken, corn, peas, basil and sage
  5. Cook uncovered, for 4-5 minutes over medium heat or until heated through
  6. Stir in the rice, salt and pepper
  7. Get ready to eat!!
