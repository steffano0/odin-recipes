Avocado Crab Boats

Description:

A healthy snack for every ocation, you can replace the crab meat with chicken or meat of your preference.

Ingredients:

  • 5 medium ripe avocados, peeled and halved
  • 1/2 cup mayonnaise
  • 2 tablespoons lemon juice
  • 2 cans (6 ounces each) lump crabmeat, drained
  • 4 tablespoons chopped fresh cilantro, divided
  • 2 tablespoons minced chives
  • 1 serrano pepper, seeded and minced
  • 1 tablespoon capers, drained
  • 1/4 teaspoon pepper
  • 1 cup shredded pepper jack cheese
  • 1/2 teaspoon paprika
  • Lemon wedges

Steps:

  1. Preheat broiler
  2. Place 2 avocaldo halves in a large bowl and mash lightly with a fork
  3. Add mayonnaise and lemon juice; mix until well blended
  4. Stir in crabe meat (meat), 3 tablespoons cilantro, chives, serrano pepper, capers and pepper
  5. Spoon into remaining avocado halves
  6. Transfer to a baking pan
  7. Sprinkle with cheese and paprika
  8. Broil 4-5 in. From heat until cheese is melted, 3-5 min
  9. Sprinkle with remaining cilantro
  10. Get ready to eat!!
